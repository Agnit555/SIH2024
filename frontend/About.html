About Us

Transport

Transportation plays a crucial role in an Assured Contract Farming System, particularly in ensuring stable market access. Efficient transportation systems help in the timely delivery of agricultural produce from farms to markets or processing units, reducing post-harvest losses and maintaining product quality.

Market

In an Assured Contract Farming System, the market is a critical component that guarantees stable access for farmers to sell their produce. The market serves as the endpoint where agricultural products are bought and sold, often under pre-agreed terms specified in the contract.

Seller

In an Assured Contract Farming System, the seller is typically the farmer or a group of farmers who agree to cultivate and supply a specific quantity and quality of agricultural produce to a buyer, often a company or cooperative.

Buyer

In an Assured Contract Farming System, the buyer is usually a company, cooperative, or agribusiness entity that enters into an agreement with farmers to purchase their agricultural produce.
